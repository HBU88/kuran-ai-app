Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 3, 2026 App: HAKAI (iOS)

Summary: HAKAI does not require account registration. Location is used only on-device for prayer times and Qibla direction. Chat messages are processed to generate AI responses and are not stored on our servers.

1. Introduction

HAKAI ("we", "our", "the app") is an iOS application developed by Burak Uckun that provides Islamic guidance services including religious Q&A (ilmihal), Quranic verse recommendations, daily prayer times, and a Qibla compass.

This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use HAKAI and how it is handled. By using the app, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

Data TypePurposeStorage
Location (latitude/longitude)Prayer times & Qibla directionOn-device only
Chat messagesAI response generation (OpenAI API)Not stored on our servers
Anonymous device identifierMonthly usage quota trackingServer — anonymous
App preferencesNotification & language settingsOn-device (UserDefaults)

We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, payment details, or any personally identifiable information.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Location — solely to calculate prayer times and Qibla direction on your device. Never transmitted to our servers.
  • Chat messages — forwarded to OpenAI's API to generate responses. Not logged or stored by HAKAI.
  • Anonymous device ID — used to enforce a monthly free usage quota. Contains no personal information.
  • App preferences — stored locally to remember your notification and display settings.

4. Data Storage & Security

All network communication uses HTTPS / TLS 1.2–1.3. Authentication tokens are stored in the iOS Keychain. On-device data is protected by iOS Data Protection (AES-256).

The anonymous device identifier stored on our server contains no personal information and is reset automatically each calendar month.

5. Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI API — Chat content is forwarded to OpenAI to generate responses. See OpenAI Privacy Policy.
  • Diyanet API — Prayer times are fetched using a city name only; no location coordinates are transmitted.
  • Apple StoreKit — In-app support purchases are handled entirely by Apple. HAKAI never receives or stores payment information.

6. Children's Privacy

HAKAI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights

  • Location access — revoke anytime in iOS Settings → Privacy → Location Services → HAKAI.
  • Device ID deletion — request removal by contacting us at the email below.
  • Data access — request information about data associated with your device ID.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be announced via an in-app notification or by updating the effective date above. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
